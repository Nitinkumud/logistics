At Eazeeship, we are committed to reducing our environmental impact and investing in green technologies. Here are some highlights on our journey to a more sustainable future for all.

01

Carbon
Offsetting

Eazeeship offsets greenhouse gas emissions from last-mile deliveries by investing in third-party verified carbon-offset projects. In Canada, Eazeeship partners with Carbonzero for GHG measurement, reduction, and carbon-offsetting solutions. Projects include the B.C. Greenhouse Biomass to Energy Project in Abbotsford, British Columbia and the Ontario Greenhouse Biomass to Energy Project, in Leamington and Flamborough, Ontario.

02

Verified
Reforestation

Eazeeship partners with veritree to enhance its sustainability initiatives through verified reforestation, planting a verified tree for every 200 mid-mile kilometres driven. This collaboration supports the restoration of the Cariboo Region in British Columbia, which has been significantly impacted by wildfires.
